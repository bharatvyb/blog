Designing Products That Respect Attention

Technology should serve focus, not fracture it.

Attention is our most valuable cognitive resource.

Modern interfaces are often designed to capture and retain it at any cost.

Respectful product design creates calm interactions, reduces friction, and gives users control over their time and energy.

This is a design philosophy BharatVyb is committed to.
